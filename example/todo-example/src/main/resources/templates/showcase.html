Choose Your Style

Use DSL when you want compile-time safety, IDE refactoring support, and type-safe query building. Perfect for applications where maintainability and refactoring are priorities.

Use Templates when you have complex existing SQL, need full SQL expressiveness (window functions, CTEs), or are migrating from MyBatis. Templates also support dynamic directives like @if, @for, and @where.
